LIVING/BIOTIC COMPONENTS

It is the community of various organisms, plants, animals and microbes comprising many different interdependent populations. Depending upon their mode of nutrition.Biotic components, or biotic factors, can be described as any living component that affects another organism or shapes the ecosystem. This includes both animals that consume other organisms within their ecosystem, and the organism that is being consumed.

The biotic components of an ecosystem is primarily made up of three kinds of organisms-

  • PRODUCERS
  • CONSUMERS
  • DECOMPOSERS
